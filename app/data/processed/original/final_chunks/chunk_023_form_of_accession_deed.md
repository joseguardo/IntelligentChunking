# Definitions

"Acquisition" means the acquisition by the Company of the Target Shares on the terms of the Acquisition Documents;

"Acquisition Agreement" means the agreement dated on or about the date of this agreement relating to the sale and purchase of the Target Shares and made between the Company and the Vendor;

"Acquisition Costs" means all fees, costs and expenses, stamp, registration and other Taxes incurred by the Parent or any other Group Company in connection with the Acquisition or the Transaction Documents;

"Acquisition Documents" means the Acquisition Agreement, the Disclosure Letter, any related warranty and indemnity agreement (or similar) if any and any other document designated as an "Acquisition Document" by the Facility Agent and the Parent;
