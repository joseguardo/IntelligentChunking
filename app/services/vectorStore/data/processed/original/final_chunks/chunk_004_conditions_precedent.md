# 12.10 Application of Prepayments

Any prepayment of a Utilisation (other than a prepayment pursuant to clause 10.1 (Illegality) or clause 10.5 (Right of Cancellation and Repayment in Relation to a Single Lender)) shall be applied pro rata to each Lender's participation in that Utilisation.
